Power Pack Protocol Bulletin

T.D. Williamson, Inc.

August 2017

Problem

Field technicians do not install power packs (industrial batteries) properly, leading to failed pipeline inspection runs and lost revenue.

Objective

Design a graphical bulletin and reference sheet for explaining the proper protocol for installing power packs in pipeline inspection tools.

Outcome

The final product features a highly refined, systematic presentation of the installation protocol. It also includes a reference sheet that streamlines the process of calculating the quantity of power packs needed given the type of power pack, the length of the inspection run, and the current draw of the tool.
