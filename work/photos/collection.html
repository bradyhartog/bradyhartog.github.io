CØLLECTIØN

2015–Present

CØLLECTIØN contains my favorite photographs from my collective personal experiences. These photographs possess the highest degree of aesthetic or personal significance to myself according to the time they were taken. They span a broad range of subject matter: portraits, landscapes, still lifes, abstractions; natural and architectural, spontaneous and contrived, minimal and detailed, balanced and extreme. The process of making these photographs serves not only as an exercise for improving my image-making skills, but as a time capsule for some of the most interesting and meaningful moments during that process and during my life at large.

As an ongoing endeavor, CØLLECTIØN is sorted by year. In principle, successive years should reveal gradual refinements in the quality, consistency and scope of my image-making along with subtly shifting subject matter as my interests and locations evolve.

While I intend that my images are constantly improving, those which I consider the most successful are not always the most recent. As a result of combining the practical and the personal, the most successful images transpire at elusive moments when time and intention interact in beautifully unexpected ways. I believe this kind of interaction occurs constantly, if rarely, in the practice of image-making. As a result, I hope that during every year CØLLECTIØN has attained some such success, and that in doing so, it imparts some of the same joy and intrigue that I have experienced in creating it.
