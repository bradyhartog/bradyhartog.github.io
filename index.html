UFLA Poster

Two iterations of a poster made for the Utah Foreign Language Association (UFLA) and its upcoming conference, based on the theme, "With Languages, the Sky's the Limit." The artwork explores the symbolism of the sky and of reaching new heights, all while maintaining an extremely minimal aesthetic.

Christmas Season Art

Artwork developed for the Christmas season sermon series—"Wise Men Still Seek Him"—at South Mountain Community Church. The symbolism of stars was used to maintain consistency with the Christmas concert artwork (see next) as well as with the theme itself.

Christmas in Utah

A series of posters (as part of a larger body of graphics) for South Mountain Community Church's annual Christmas concert, featuring original hand lettering. Both pure vector geometry and raster images were used to develop the graphics. They make interesting use of the Milky Way as a texture, rather than a background image.

Utah HOSA Graphic

A graphic developed for use on T-shirts, jackets, and various other materials throughout the year for the Hillcrest chapter of Utah HOSA (Health Occupations Students of America). It features a radial gradient (which leverages a halftone technique for use in screen-printing) that draws emphasis to the text at center.

Vision 2016 Artwork

Artwork for the Vision 2016 sermon series at South Mountain Community Church. Shown here is the lobby poster. Based on an original photograph of the Grand Canyon of the Yellowstone.

Lorem Ipsum Posters

Experimental poster designs. The left specimen explores the use of type illustration to guide a layout. The center specimen combines a complementary color scheme with bold imagery to make a particularly strong impression. The right specimen explores the use of the fundamental CMYK colors to create a diverse yet cohesive appearance.

Lorem Ipsum Posters

Experimental poster designs. The left specimen explores the use of minimalistic photography to create an "atmosphere" for information. The center specimen explores the use of a monochrome color scheme combined with minimal geometry. The right specimen explores similar ideas as that of the left.

members3 Brand

Brand developed for the members3 ("Members Cubed: Then, Now, Tomorrow") program. The design is clean, modern, and forward-looking. The cool-end color scheme has a professional attitude and is extremely versatile.

Hillcrest Music Brand

Brand developed for Hillcrest Music Outreach. The logo was evolved from the intersection between a pair of eighth notes and a paw (to represent a husky, the group's mascot).

UACTE Logo Diagram

A diagram illustrating how the UACTE 2017 logo was constructed. It provides insight into the theory behind the design as well.

UACTE Conference Art

Conference artwork created for the 2017 Utah Association for Career and Technical Education annual conference. Shown here is the logo and color scheme, developed from the theme, "Grow Your Own."

Vertex Brand

An experimental brand for the prospective startup Vertex. It features a grayscale color scheme with subtle organic textures and a fully custom logotype.

Cloudalize Values

A poster made to display the company values of Cloudalize. The bold style and dynamic layout create a sense of motion, resulting in a work that is fun and engaging. It reflects the Cloudalize brand in every detail, most notably the color scheme.

bradyhartog Icons

Hand-drawn icons used as part of my brand to represent various disciplines of design. There is one for each element of the color scheme, helping to create a lasting visual impression.

bradyhartog Brand

My personal brand. Developed under a strictly minimal vision. The logo takes on a full as well as a condensed form for optimal legibility across a variety of situations. The CMYK color scheme suggests creativity and originality.
